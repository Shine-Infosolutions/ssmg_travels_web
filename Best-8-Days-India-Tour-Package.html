Tour Overview

Explore the cultural, historical, and spiritual heart of India with this Best 8 Days India Tour Package by SSMG Travels, Gorakhpur. This tour covers India’s most iconic destinations, including Varanasi, Agra, Delhi, and Jaipur. Experience the divine Ganga Aarti, the majestic Taj Mahal, and the royal history of Rajasthan in one comfortable and well-planned road journey.

Tour Highlights:
  • Spiritual experience in Varanasi with Ganga Aarti and Sarnath visit.
  • Iconic Taj Mahal and Agra Fort exploration.
  • Heritage monuments of Delhi including Red Fort and India Gate.
  • Royal experience in the Pink City of Jaipur with Amber Fort visit.
  • Day-wise Itinerary
  • Inclusions
Day 01: Gorakhpur → Varanasi

Morning pickup from Gorakhpur Railway Station / Gorakhpur Airport and drive to Varanasi. In the evening, witness the divine Ganga Aarti at Dashashwamedh Ghat. Overnight stay in Varanasi.

Day 02: Varanasi Sightseeing

Early morning boat ride on River Ganga. Visit Kashi Vishwanath Temple, Sarnath (Buddhist site), and local temples. Overnight stay in Varanasi.

Day 03: Varanasi → Agra

Proceed for a long scenic drive to the city of love, Agra. Arrival and hotel check-in. Rest and leisure time. Overnight stay in Agra.

Day 04: Agra Sightseeing → Delhi

Visit the world-famous Taj Mahal and the historic Agra Fort. Later in the afternoon, drive to the capital city, Delhi. Overnight stay in Delhi.

Day 05: Delhi Sightseeing

Explore Old & New Delhi: India Gate, Qutub Minar, Lotus Temple, Red Fort, and Jama Masjid. Stroll through the local markets. Overnight stay in Delhi.

Day 06: Delhi → Jaipur

Drive to Jaipur, the Pink City of Rajasthan. Evening at leisure for local shopping and exploring Jaipur's vibrant streets. Overnight stay in Jaipur.

Day 07: Jaipur Sightseeing

Full day Jaipur sightseeing: Amber Fort, City Palace, Hawa Mahal, and Jantar Mantar. Experience the rich Rajasthani culture. Overnight stay in Jaipur.

Day 08: Jaipur → Gorakhpur Return

Early morning start for the return journey towards Gorakhpur. Tour ends with unforgettable memories of Heritage India.
