Tour Overview

SSMG Travels, Gorakhpur ke saath Kathmandu ka ek short aur memorable road tour enjoy karein. Yeh 3 din ka Kathmandu tour package un logon ke liye perfect hai jo bina flight, sirf road journey ke madhyam se Nepal ki cultural aur religious heritage explore karna chahte hain. Kathmandu Nepal ki rajdhani hai, jo apne ancient temples aur UNESCO heritage sites ke liye mashhoor hai.

Tour Highlights:
  • Safe road journey from Gorakhpur to the heart of Nepal.
  • Visit top religious sites like Pashupatinath and Boudhanath Stupa.
  • Short, budget-friendly & comfortable tour for families and groups.
  • Explore the rich ancient heritage and local markets of Kathmandu.
  • Day-wise Itinerary
  • Inclusions
Day 01: Gorakhpur → Kathmandu

Subah Gorakhpur se pickup. Sonauli border ke through Nepal entry. Shaam ko Kathmandu pahunchna aur hotel check-in. Evening free for leisure. Overnight stay in Kathmandu.

Day 02: Kathmandu Sightseeing

Kathmandu sightseeing – Pashupatinath Temple, Boudhanath Stupa, Swayambhunath (Monkey Temple), Kathmandu Durbar Square. Evening free for local market exploration. Overnight stay in Kathmandu.

Day 03: Kathmandu → Gorakhpur Departure

Subah return journey start. After completing border formalities, drop-off at Gorakhpur in the evening. Tour ends.

What’s Included
  • Pickup & Drop from Gorakhpur
  • Road Transportation (Car/Bus)
  • Sightseeing as per Itinerary
  • Driver Allowance, Fuel & Toll
What’s Not Included
  • Hotel Accommodations & Meals
  • Entry Tickets & Personal Expenses
