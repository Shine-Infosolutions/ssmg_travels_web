Tour Overview

Experience the ultimate 11-day overland expedition curated by SSMG Travels. This journey takes you from the gateway of Gorakhpur into the heart of Nepal, covering the peace of Lumbini, the scenic lakes of Pokhara, the wild terrains of Chitwan, and the heritage of Kathmandu. The return journey includes a divine visit to the holy city of Ayodhya and the ancient ghats of Varanasi. Travel with the comfort of an AC vehicle and the freedom to choose your final drop-off location in either Varanasi or Gorakhpur.

  • 11 Days Itinerary
  • Inclusions
Day 01: Gorakhpur Pickup and Departure

Pickup from Gorakhpur City. Scenic drive towards the Nepal border and arrival at Lumbini. Evening at leisure. Overnight in Lumbini.

Day 02: Lumbini Sightseeing

Visit the Maya Devi Temple, Ashokan Pillar, and explore international monastic zones in the peaceful Lumbini Garden. Overnight in Lumbini.

Day 03: Lumbini to Pokhara Valley

Drive into the hills towards Pokhara. In the evening, enjoy a relaxing walk along the serene Fewa Lake. Overnight in Pokhara.

Day 04: Pokhara Sightseeing

Sunrise views at Sarangkot, visit Davis Falls, Gupteshwor Cave, and enjoy boating at the Fewa Lake to reach Tal Barahi Temple. Overnight in Pokhara.

Day 05: Pokhara Leisure and Adventure

A free day for self-exploration or optional adventure activities like Paragliding, Zip-lining, or mountain biking. Overnight in Pokhara.

Day 06: Pokhara to Chitwan National Park

Drive to the heart of the Terai region. Evening highlights include a traditional Tharu cultural dance program. Overnight in Chitwan.

Day 07: Chitwan Wildlife Safari

Morning Jeep or Elephant safari in the National Park, Canoe ride on the Rapti River, and a guided nature walk. Overnight in Chitwan.

Day 08: Chitwan to Kathmandu Capital

Drive to Kathmandu. Optional stop at Manakamana Temple via cable car. Evening exploration of Thamel market. Overnight in Kathmandu.

Day 09: Kathmandu Heritage Tour

Full day visit to Pashupatinath Temple, Boudhanath Stupa, and Kathmandu Durbar Square. Traditional Nepali dinner in the evening. Overnight in Kathmandu.

Day 10: Kathmandu to Ayodhya (India Return)

Travel back into India. Visit the holy city of Ayodhya, including Ram Janmabhoomi and Hanuman Garhi. Overnight in Ayodhya.

Day 11: Ayodhya to Varanasi and Drop-off (Your Choice)

Proceed to Varanasi for optional sightseeing. Final drop-off at your preferred location in Varanasi City or Gorakhpur. Tour ends.
