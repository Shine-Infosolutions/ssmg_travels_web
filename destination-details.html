The SSMG Travels Promise

At Maa Vindhyavasini Tour & Transport, we go beyond standard travel. Rooted in the rich cultural heritage of Nepal, we are passionate about sharing the soul of this land. Our mission is simple: To connect people through meaningful, authentic, and unforgettable experiences.

  • Detailed Itinerary
  • Inclusions
Day 01: Gorakhpur - Lumbini - Kathmandu

Departure from Gorakhpur Junction/Airport. Seek blessings at Gorakhnath Temple. Scenic drive to Sonauli Border. Visit the birthplace of Lord Buddha in Lumbini before heading towards Kathmandu's vibrant streets.

Day 02: Spiritual Heart of Kathmandu

Explore Pashupatinath, Guhyeshwari Temple, and the colossal Boudhanath Stupa. Witness the Sleeping Vishnu at Buddhanilkantha and finish with sunset views at Swayambhunath (Monkey Temple).

Day 03: Manakamna Darshan & Departure

Soulful visit to the wish-fulfilling Manakamna Temple via Cable Car. Drive back to Gorakhpur for station/airport drop-off with cherished Nepal memories.
