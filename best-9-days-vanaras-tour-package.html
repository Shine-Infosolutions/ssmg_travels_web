Tour Overview

Embark on a captivating 9-day overland journey from the spiritual city of Varanasi to the sacred landscapes of Nepal and the historic streets of Ayodhya. This carefully curated package by SSMG Travels blends culture, spirituality, and adventure, from the holy Ganga Ghats and Sarnath in Varanasi to the tranquil Maya Devi Temple in Lumbini. Experience the breathtaking beauty of Pokhara’s lakes and Himalayan views, the wild charm of Chitwan National Park, and the rich heritage of Kathmandu and Manakamana Temple. The tour concludes with immersive sightseeing in Ayodhya, with flexible drop-off options at either Varanasi or Gorakhpur for your convenience.

  • The Itinerary
  • Inclusions
Day 01:Varanasi – Pickup & Local Sightseeing

Start your journey with a convenient pickup from your hotel, railway station, or airport in Varanasi. Explore the spiritual city with visits to Kashi Vishwanath Temple, Dashashwamedh Ghat, and Assi Ghat. Enjoy an optional boat ride on the Ganga River and witness the magical Ganga Aarti in the evening. Overnight stay in Varanasi hotel.

Day 02: Varanasi to Lumbini (Nepal Border Crossing)

Drive from Varanasi to the Nepal border (Sonauli / Belahia) and continue your journey to the sacred city of Lumbini, the birthplace of Lord Buddha. Spend the evening relaxing and exploring the local surroundings. Overnight stay in Lumbini hotel.

Day 03: Lumbini Sightseeing & Drive to Pokhara

Begin the day with visits to Maya Devi Temple, Ashokan Pillar, and the numerous monasteries in Lumbini. After sightseeing, drive to Pokhara, enjoying the scenic landscapes en route. Overnight stay in Pokhara hotel.

Day 04:Pokhara Sightseeing

Experience the natural beauty of Pokhara with a Sarangkot sunrise view of the Himalayas. Visit Davis Falls, Gupteshwor Cave, and the World Peace Pagoda. Spend the evening relaxing at Lakeside. Overnight stay in Pokhara hotel.

Day 05: Pokhara to Chitwan National Park

Drive to Chitwan National Park and enjoy a peaceful evening exploring the local area or cultural activities. Overnight stay in Chitwan hotel.

Day 06: Chitwan Sightseeing & Drive to Kathmandu via Manakamana

Start the day with a jungle safari, elephant ride, or bird watching in Chitwan National Park. Later, drive to Kathmandu, visiting the sacred Manakamana Temple en route. Overnight stay in Kathmandu hotel.

Day 07: Kathmandu Sightseeing

Explore the cultural heart of Nepal by visiting Pashupatinath Temple, Boudhanath Stupa, and Kathmandu Durbar Square. Optional cultural programs can be enjoyed in the evening. Overnight stay in Kathmandu hotel.p>

Day 08: Kathmandu – Ayodhya

Drive from Kathmandu to Ayodhya, arriving in time for a relaxed evening. Explore the local market or enjoy leisure activities. Overnight stay in Ayodhya hotel.

Day 09: Ayodhya Sightseeing & Passenger Choice Drop

Spend the day exploring the spiritual city with visits to Ram Janmabhoomi Temple, Hanuman Garhi, and other local temples. In the evening, passengers can choose their drop-off location: Gorakhpur or Varanasi, ending the memorable tour.
